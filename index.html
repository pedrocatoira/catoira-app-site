About

Two crafts, one steady hand

I'm Pedro — an agile coach and hands-on app developer. Most of my clients need one of two things: a team that's stuck and needs a clearer way of working, or an idea that needs to become a real, working product. I do both, and I do them properly.

On the coaching side, I've spent over a decade inside engineering organizations — as a Scrum Master, an engineering manager, and a coach — helping teams move from chaotic sprints to a rhythm they actually trust. I don't sell frameworks for their own sake. I look at how a team actually works, then help them fix what's slowing them down.

On the development side, I build and ship apps end-to-end: product strategy, design decisions, iOS and web builds, and the unglamorous work of getting something through App Store review and into people's hands. Catoira Consulting exists because founders and teams usually need both a plan and someone who can execute it.

How it works

The voyage, in four legs

Whether the work is coaching or a build, the shape of the engagement is the same — a short, honest process with clear waypoints.

01

Chart

A working session to map where things stand — the team's friction points, or the app's real requirements. No assumptions.

02

Set sail

A concrete plan: a coaching cadence with clear goals, or a scoped build with milestones you can hold me to.

03

Navigate

The actual work — coaching sessions and process changes, or design, development and testing cycles. Regular check-ins throughout.

04

Make harbor

A team that's found its rhythm, or an app that's live and in users' hands — plus a clear handoff or ongoing support plan.
